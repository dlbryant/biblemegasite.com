WHAT I THINK OF THE APOCRYPHA



First of all, I'm not a member of the churches that use the Apocrypha. While I don't believe that the Apocrypha was inspired by God as Holy Scripture like the 66 books of the Bible were, the Apocrypha was written between the time after Malachi was written and before the birth of Christ. It gives a great look into those times, both in literature, beliefs, and history. While the Apocrypha is not God-inspired scripture, it is well worth reading.
