Jesus is God

The following list of the "37 Proofs of the Deity of Jesus Christ," is from the "Book of Bible Lists" by H.L. Willmington, Tyndale House Publishers, Inc., Wheaton, IL, 1987.

37 Proofs of the Deity of Jesus Christ
1 He was omnipotent over disease Matthew 8:1-4 Luke 4:39
2 He was omnipotent over demons Matthew 8:16-17 Matthew 8:28-32 Luke 4:35
3 He was omnipotent over men Matthew 9:9 John 17:2
4 He was omnipotent over nature Matthew 8:26
5 He was omnipotent over sin Matthew 9:1-8
6 He was omnipotent over traditions Matthew 9:10-17
7 He was omnipotent over death Luke 7:14-15 Luke 8:54-56 John 11:4
8 He was omniscient, re. Nathanael John 1:48
9 He was omniscient, re. Judas' plot John 6:70
10 He was omniscient, re. Pharisees' hearts Matthew 12:25 Luke 5:22 Luke 6:8 Luke 7:39-40
11 He knew the thoughts of the scribes Matthew 9:3-4
12 He knew the sincerity of one scribe Matthew 12:34
13 He knew the sincerity of the Samaritan woman John 4:24
14 He knew the diciples' problems Luke 9:46-47
15 He was omnipresent Matthew 18:20 Matthew 28:20 John 3:13 John 14:20
16 He was worshiped as God by the angels Hebrews 1:6
17 He was worshiped as God by the shepherds Luke 2:15
18 He was worshiped as God by the wise men Matthew 2:2 Matthew 2:11
19 He was worshiped as God by a leper Matthew 8:2
20 He was worshiped as God by a ruler Matthew 9:18
21 He was worshiped as God by a Canaanite woman Matthew 15:25
22 He was worshiped as God by a mother Matthew 20:20
23 He was worshiped as God by a maniac Mark 5:6
24 He was worshiped as God by a man born blind John 9:38
25 He was worshiped as God by Thomas John 20:28
26 He was worshiped as God by some Greeks John 12:20-21
27 He was worshiped as God by his apostles Matthew 14:33 Matthew 28:9
28 He forgave sins Mark 2:5 Mark 2:10-11
29 He judges John 5:22
30 He saves Matthew 18:11 John 10:28
31 Stephen called him God Acts 7:59
32 The eunuch called him God Acts 8:37
33 Paul called him God Galatians 2:20 Colossians 1:15-17 Colossians 2:9 1 Timothy 3:16 Titus 2:13
34 Peter called him God 1 Peter 3:22 1 Peter 1:17
35 Jude called him God Jude 25
36 James called him God James 2:1
37 John called him God 1 John 5:20 Revelation 1:18 Revelation 19:16
